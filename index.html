Short description

Locked-In is a lightweight focus tracker built with Google Teachable Machine (PoseNet). It classifies the user's posture and gaze into Focused, Distracted, or Taking A Break. The tracker runs locally in your browser and provides audible and visual alerts when distraction is detected (based on your sensitivity setting).

How the model works

The model extracts body keypoints (head, shoulders, etc.) using PoseNet and classifies the pose pattern into one of three labels. The highest-probability class determines the live state. You can tune the sensitivity slider to adjust how confidently "Distracted" must be detected before an alert is triggered.

Privacy & safety

All processing happens locally in your browser. No webcam footage is uploaded. You can stop the webcam at any time with the Stop button or close the page to end processing.
